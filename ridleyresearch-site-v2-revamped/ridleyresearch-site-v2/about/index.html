About Ridley Research

There's a leverage gap in financial services, and it's been there for years.

Large firms have 50-person ops teams, dedicated tech stacks, and entire departments for things solo advisors and small firms handle alone — or don't handle at all. That gap used to be a given. A cost of doing business at a certain scale.

Then something changed.

I started watching AI agents reach a point where one person, coordinating the right system, could operate at the scale of a department. Not someday. Now. The tools were real, the results were real, and most people in financial services had no idea it was happening.

That's why I built Ridley Research.

We help independent advisors and small financial services firms close the leverage gap — without the headcount. No enterprise budget required. No year-long implementation. Just a tightly built system that works while you focus on clients.

I do this work because I've seen what it looks like when someone gets it right. Clients who came in overwhelmed and left with infrastructure they didn't think was possible for a firm their size.

One of them put it this way:

"Couldn't have gotten this going without Deacon. He fast tracked getting me through set up hurdles and helped me get going with this amazing tool. Such a huge help."

— Andrew Simmons

If you're tired of being outgunned by firms with bigger teams and bigger budgets, this is what we do.
